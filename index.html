ABOUT

With a blend of creativity and data, I am a versatile professional with diverse skills that make me an asset for a myriad of tasks and projects. With over a decade of experience, my background includes administrative and project coordination, operations support, as well as creative and data reporting capabilities.

From supporting executives at Fortune 500 companies to creating impactful content and visualizing data insights, I thrive in dynamic environments where I can wear multiple hats and contribute to strategic success. Each experience has enriched my skillset and fueled my passion for turning ideas into impactful realities.

Here's a glimpse into my adventure:

CREATIVE: After college, I dove into digital media, building WordPress sites, designing graphics, and exploring multimedia. I gained a strong appreciation for visual storytelling and user-centered design, skills I now use in creating strategic, visually compelling content.

ADMINISTRATION: In administrative roles, I discovered a talent for organizing chaos. From managing complex schedules to coordinating events and creating client-facing materials, I created a toolkit for prioritizing, organizing, and collaborating with diverse teams.

MARKETING & CONTENT STRATEGY: Marketing drew me in, and I created proposals that helped secure millions in contracts, developed presentations, and collaborated on managing content across channels like email and social media. I also honed skills in SEO and content optimization for the web.

DATA VISUALIZATION: Driven by a need for deeper understanding, I developed my data analytics and visualization skills, completing the Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate. I further developed my skills in Microsoft Excel and Google Spreadsheets and learned more about Tableau and SQL. Now, I speak the language of analyzing numbers, visualizing trends, and using data to tell compelling stories.
