ABOUT

With a blend of creativity and data, I am a professional with diverse skills that make me a versatile asset for a myriad of tasks and projects. With over a decade of experience, my background includes marketing and administrative coordination, web and graphic design, web content management, and data visualization.

From supporting executives at Fortune 500 companies to creating various marketing collateral and analyzing and visualizing data, I thrive in dynamic environments where I can wear multiple hats and contribute to strategic success. Each experience has enriched my skillset and fueled my passion for turning ideas into impactful realities.

Here's a glimpse into my adventure:

CREATIVE: After college, I spent my days with pixels and digital paintbrushes. I built WordPress sites, designed graphics in Photoshop, and dabbled in multimedia design. I gained a strong appreciation for visual storytelling and user-centered design through this experience.

ADMINISTRATION: Through working in administrative roles, I discovered I enjoy organizing chaos. As an administrative assistant, I juggled complex schedules, coordinated events, collaborated with professionals on presentation slide decks, reports, and other client-facing marketing material, and kept teams running smoothly. My ability to prioritize, organize, and collaborate became my secret weapon.

MARKETING: The marketing world beckoned, and I answered. I created proposals resulting in millions in contracts, crafted presentations, collaborated on email marketing, social media marketing, and website management, and mastered website SEO.

DATA VISUALIZATION: Fueled by a thirst for deeper understanding, I developed my data analytics and visualization skills by completing the Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate. I further developed my skills in Microsoft Excel and Google Spreadsheets and learned more about Tableau and SQL. Now, I speak the language of analyzing numbers, visualizing trends, and using data to tell compelling stories.
